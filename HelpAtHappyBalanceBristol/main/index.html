We are Claire and SJ

Together we are Happy Balance declutterers

Happy Balance is based in Easton In Gordano BS20 and covers Bristol and North Somerset. To schedule a consultation please contact us via email, phone call, whatsapp, instagram or facebook.

We are declutterers

Happy Balance, Claire and SJ, are declutterers and an additional pair of hands to help you:

• Declutter

• Organise your home and help you create space

• Prepare your home for a move/rental/visitors

• Sort and prepare an estate so that it is ready for sale

Happy Balance declutterers can help you regain control and save you time if you are:

• Downsizing, moving house and running out of time?

• Need physical and emotional support to sort the property of a deceased relative or non-family member?

• Realising you have no time for managing your home – you have a loft, garage, playroom or house that needs decluttering and sorting?

• Working full-time which means you have no time to declutter and organise as weekends with family and friends takes priority?

• Just feeling overwhelmed and need some help?

• Wanting to recycle wherever possible and work with companies that up-cycle unwanted items
